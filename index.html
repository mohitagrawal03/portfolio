    HanoiHelper

    The Puzzle-Solving Prodigy

    HanoiHelper is an innovative automation project that leverages the Tower of Hanoi puzzle to demonstrate advanced algorithmic and robotic capabilities. It features a precise servo-controlled arm executing the recursive algorithm to solve the puzzle. Showcases the potential of automation in industrial applications, from pick-and-drop tasks to complex assembly lines.

    SpotSense

    Intelligent Stage Spotlight System

    SpotSense is an innovative stage lighting solution designed to automate and enhance the control of spotlights. Leveraging ultrasonic sensors for real-time positioning, the system utilizes Arduino Uno, servo motors, and spotlights to provide precise and adaptable lighting. Easily installable and cost-effective, SpotSense transforms manual spotlight adjustments into a seamless automated process.
